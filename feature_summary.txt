                       BILLSUTRA — COMPLETE FEATURE SUMMARY

Generated: 2026-03-10

BillSutra is a full-stack business management and billing platform for small and
medium businesses. It covers end-to-end workflows: invoicing, purchases, sales,
inventory, payments, analytics, and reporting — all in one place.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. AUTHENTICATION & USER MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Email/Password Registration with bcrypt hashing (cost factor 12)
- OAuth Login (Google/Provider) via NextAuth on the front-end + JWT token exchange
  with the backend
- JWT Tokens with 365-day expiry for API authentication
- Forgot Password: generates a crypto random token (valid 30 minutes), stored in DB
- Reset Password: validates token, updates hashed password, marks token as used
- Profile Update: change name, email
- Password Update: update password from profile settings
- Auth-protected routes: Bearer token middleware on all private API routes
- Front-end route protection: NextAuth JWT middleware blocks unauthenticated
  access to /dashboard/** routes and redirects to sign-in with callback URL

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. BUSINESS PROFILE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Create/Update business profile (upsert): business name, address, phone, email,
  website, logo URL, tax ID (GST), currency (default: INR)
- Toggle: show/hide logo on invoice, show/hide tax number, show/hide payment QR
- Business profile information is embedded in generated invoice PDFs

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Full CRUD for customers (create, read, update, delete)
- Customer fields: name, email, phone, address
- Customers linked to invoices, sales, and recurring invoice templates
- Top customer analytics: revenue contribution and number of orders

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Full CRUD for suppliers
- Supplier fields: name, email, phone, address
- Suppliers linked to purchase orders
- Dashboard supplier analytics: pending payables per supplier

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. PRODUCT & CATEGORY MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Full CRUD for products
- Product fields: name, SKU (unique per user), barcode (globally unique), selling
  price, cost price, GST rate (default 18%), stock on hand, reorder level,
  category
- Full CRUD for product categories
- Category uniqueness enforced per user (user_id + name)
- Products linked to: purchase items, sale items, invoice items, stock movements,
  inventory records

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. WAREHOUSE & INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Full CRUD for warehouses (name, location)
- Inventory records: tracks quantity of each product in each warehouse
- Inventory query: filter by warehouse
- Inventory adjustment: manually increase/decrease stock with a reason
- Stock movements tracked as audit trail: PURCHASE, SALE, ADJUSTMENT, RETURN, DAMAGE
- Low-stock and out-of-stock alerts based on reorder level

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. PURCHASE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Create/Update/Read purchases with multiple line items
- Purchase fields: supplier, warehouse, purchase date, subtotal, tax, total,
  notes, line items (product, name, quantity, unit cost, tax rate, line total)
- Payment tracking on purchases: total amount, paid amount, pending amount,
  payment status (PAID / PARTIALLY_PAID / UNPAID), payment date, payment method
- Payment methods: CASH, CARD, BANK_TRANSFER, UPI, CHEQUE, OTHER
- Stock automatically updated when a purchase is recorded
- Dashboard shows pending supplier payables

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. SALES MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Full CRUD for sales
- Sale fields: customer (optional - walk-in supported), sale date, items
  (product, name, qty, unit price, tax rate, line total), subtotal, tax, total,
  notes
- Sale status: DRAFT, COMPLETED, VOID
- Payment tracking on sales: similar to purchases (total, paid, pending, status)
- Invoice-to-sale sync: creating an invoice with sync_sales=true auto-creates a
  corresponding sale record and decrements inventory

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. INVOICING SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Create invoices with: customer, issue date, due date, discount, notes, items
- Invoice statuses: DRAFT, SENT, PARTIALLY_PAID, PAID, OVERDUE, VOID
- Auto-generated invoice numbers (sequential, per user)
- Overdue sync: at every invoice list/get request, invoices past due date
  (and not yet PAID) are automatically transitioned to OVERDUE status
- PDF generation: server-side PDF via Puppeteer (headless Chromium), A4 format,
  includes company details, customer details, itemized table, totals
- Mark as Sent: transitions invoice from DRAFT to SENT status
- Duplicate invoice: creates a new DRAFT invoice as a copy with a new number
- Delete invoice
- Invoice payments tracking: multiple partial payments per invoice
- Public invoice view: unauthenticated route (/api/public/invoice/:id) to allow
  sharing invoice links with customers
- Email notification: send invoice notification email to customer (via Nodemailer)
- Inventory sync on invoice creation: validates stock, decrements warehouse
  inventory and global stock_on_hand

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. RECURRING INVOICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Templates for recurring invoices: customer, title, items, discount, notes,
  due_in_days, frequency, interval_count
- Frequencies: DAILY, WEEKLY, MONTHLY, YEARLY with a configurable interval count
- Cron job runs at server midnight: automatically creates invoice drafts for all
  active templates whose next_run_date has passed
- After processing: last_run_date updated, next_run_date advanced by frequency

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. PAYMENT MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Record payments against invoices
- Payment fields: invoice, amount, method, provider, transaction_id, reference,
  paid_at date
- List all payments or filter by invoice
- Invoice status auto-updated after payment (PARTIALLY_PAID / PAID)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
12. INVOICE TEMPLATES & CUSTOMIZATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- System-level templates (admin controlled): layout_config, sections
- User template settings: choose a base template, enable/disable sections,
  customize theme color, section order, design config (JSON)
- User saved templates: save named custom layouts for reuse across invoices

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
13. BULK DATA IMPORT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Import module accepts CSV and XLSX file uploads (via Multer)
- Supported import types: customers, suppliers, products, categories, warehouses
- Validates each row, maps columns, inserts records in bulk
- Returns import statistics: successful, failed, error details per row

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
14. ANALYTICS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Overview analytics endpoint: aggregated business metrics
- Top selling products by quantity (last 30 days)
- Category-wise revenue breakdown
- Profit trend (monthly, rolling 6 months): revenue - purchases - expenses

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
15. DASHBOARD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The dashboard engine provides several specialized data endpoints:

  a) Overview
     - Total revenue, total purchases, expenses, receivables, payables
     - Profit metrics: today, weekly, monthly with % change vs prior period
     - Inventory value (stock_on_hand × cost/price)
     - Invoice stats: total, paid, pending, overdue counts
     - Alerts: low stock items, overdue invoices, supplier payables
     - Notifications panel (formatted alert messages)
     - Recent activity feed (last 6 sales/purchases events)
     - Pending payment rows (top 5) with amount breakdown

  b) Sales Analytics
     - Daily sales totals: last 7 days + last 30 days charts
     - Monthly comparison: sales vs purchases, last 6 months
     - Category revenue breakdown (top 5)

  c) Inventory Analytics
     - Total products, low stock count, out-of-stock count
     - Inventory value
     - Top selling product (last 30 days by units)
     - Low stock item list with stock vs reorder level

  d) Transactions
     - Last 10 sales with payment status

  e) Customer Analytics
     - Total registered customers
     - Pending payment amount
     - Customer visit breakdown: daily/weekly/monthly (registered vs walk-in)
     - Top 5 customers by revenue and order count

  f) Supplier Analytics
     - Pending payables per supplier

  g) Cash Flow
     - Inflow/outflow analysis (modes: sales, payments, or hybrid)
     - Configurable cash inflow mode

  h) Sales Forecast
     - Predictive forecast based on historical patterns and day-of-week weighting

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

18. TECHNOLOGY STACK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Front-End:
    - Next.js 16 (App Router), React 19, TypeScript
    - TailwindCSS 4, Radix UI components
    - NextAuth v4 (OAuth + credentials sessions)

    - TanStack Table for data tables

    - Lucide React icons

    - Sonner (toast notifications)
    - html2canvas, Playwright (PDF/screenshot utilities)

  Back-End (Server):
    - Node.js with Express 5, TypeScript (ESM)
    - Prisma ORM 6 with PostgreSQL
    - JWT (jsonwebtoken) for API authentication
    - bcryptjs for password hashing
    - Puppeteer for server-side PDF generation
    - Nodemailer for email notifications
    - node-cron for recurring invoice automation
    - Multer for file uploads (bulk import)
    - csv-parser + xlsx for CSV/Excel import
    - express-rate-limit for auth rate limiting
    - Morgan for HTTP request logging
    - Zod for request validation
    - CORS support

  Database Schema:
    - PostgreSQL via Prisma
    - 15 models: User, BusinessProfile, Template, TemplateSection, UserTemplate,
      UserSavedTemplate, Customer, Supplier, Category, Product, Warehouse,
      Inventory, Purchase, PurchaseItem, Sale, SaleItem, Invoice,
      RecurringInvoiceTemplate, InvoiceItem, Payment, StockMovement,
      PasswordResetToken
